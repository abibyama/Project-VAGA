Who we are

Founded in 2021, VAGA community is comprised of experts in the cosmetics industry. Vasa define well-being as going beyond financial stability to include happiness and good health. We at VASA achieve prosperity by creating and selling unique, ecological products of the highest quality.

Our Products

VAGA products are full of life and love. Each item is hand-crafted using traditional techniques, maintaining local artisanal skills such as batiking, bead-making and Shea butter production. Our products are unique and high-quality, tended to with care through a meticulous quality control process and by a dedicated customer service team.
